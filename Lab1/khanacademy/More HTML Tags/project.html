Kurry

  • Time: Approximately 40 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2
Garlick 2
Onion 1
Kurry 1
Salt as needed
Rice 320g
Cream 1

Step 1: Grill or cook chicken breast mixed with some salt and black pepper.
Step 2: Slash onion and garlick and cook with kurry powder or sauce after mixing it with cream
Step 3: Cook rice and put cooked chicken too sauce
Step 3: Mix all with this and have a great meal

SourSweet Chicken with Rice

  • Time:40 minutes
  • Serves: 3-4
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 320g
Chicken Breast 2
Salt as needed for rice
Sugar 3 table spoons
Garlick 2
Black pepper 2 table spoons
Red pepper 1 table spoons
Ginger 40g
Soy sauce 1cup
Starch 1 tablespoon
Water 1cup

Step 1: Cook Rice
Step 2: Cook chicken
Step 3: Slash ginger and garlick and put to chicken
Step 4: Put black pepper , red pepper, sugar, soy sauce
Step 5: Mix it all
Step 6: After 1 min add starch with cup of water
Step 7: Cook on 30%-40%.
Step 8: Add to Rice and Enjoy Meal
